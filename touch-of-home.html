Ingredients
  1. 2 tbsp coconut oil - divided
  2. 1 tbsp whole coriander seed
  3. 1 tbsp whole cumin seed
  4. 1/2 tsp dried mint - (optional)
  5. 1 1/2 cups whole tomatoes - (1 15 oz can)
  6. 1 small red onion - small dice, some reserved for garnish
  7. 4 cloves garlic - minced
  8. 1 cup vegetable broth
  9. 3 cups kidney beans - (2 15 oz cans), rinced and drained
  10. 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper - for taste
  11. 1 tsp salt - for taste
  12. cilantro for garnish
  13. basmati rice - to serve
