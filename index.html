Delicious Life

Where food is Life

Hey there! I'm Tina and welcome to Delicious Life! As a pre-med student, I know how hard it can be to maintain a well-balanced diet in this busy school life. When I first moved for college, I never thought I’d be using anything but the microwave in the kitchen. Yet, after craving meals that weren’t microwaveable, I began to see, feel and taste the power of making my own meals. Cooking has sweetened and spiced up my life. After my microwaveable food experience, I am a strong believer in how great meals can jazz up someone's day. I am an enthusiast in creating my own dishes. This is my outlet to express my creative passion and give y’all a taste of my delicious life!
